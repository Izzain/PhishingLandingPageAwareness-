Security Awareness Training

You have just encountered a simulated phishing link. This exercise is designed to help you identify phishing attempts and keep our company secure.

How to Avoid Phishing Attacks:

  • Verify the sender's email address and ensure it matches known contacts.
  • Be cautious if the email domain appears misspelled or altered.
  • Hover over links to check the real URL before clicking.
  • Beware of messages that pressure you to act immediately.
  • Always confirm with IT if you're in doubt about an email's legitimacy.
