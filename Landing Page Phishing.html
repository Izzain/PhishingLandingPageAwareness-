Practical Ways to Identify Fake Emails:

  • Double-check the display name. Reveal the sender address to see if it matches what you expect.
  • Take note of strange subdomains in the email address, like @accounts.hacker-portal.com.
  • Hover over any link or button to confirm the real destination before clicking.
  • Stay alert if a message urges immediate action without context. Question emails that feel rushed.
  • If in doubt, contact your company’s support or IT team for guidance.

These guidelines help you spot deception and shield company data.
